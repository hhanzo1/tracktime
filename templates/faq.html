Track Time Check-In System Guide

Ready to hit the epic RC track? Our Check-In system makes it easy to connect with fellow racers and plan awesome track time.

Before heading to the track with your gear, hop into your account and create a Check-In. This lets other racers know when you'll be there, making it easier for everyone to coordinate practice runs and races. Once you arrive, you're all set - no need to check in again!

When you Check-In, the system:

  • Shows your session on the dashboard, so other racers can see who's hitting the track and when
  • Send an optional push notification to other racers with your session details - perfect for organizing practice groups or impromptu races
  • Lets you share your Check-In on Facebook, WhatsApp, or Messages to rally your RC racing crew

Managing your track time is a breeze - edit, delete, or export your Check-Ins whenever you need to. The dashboard helps everyone coordinate their track sessions for maximum racing fun!

Stay on top of your game with:

  • A Statistics section that tracks your track time history and progress
  • Live weather updates every 15 minutes, so you know the perfect time to run your car
